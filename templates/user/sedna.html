How it Works

Find a Domain Expert!

Browse or search our community of experts to find the right one for you. We have experts from wide areas including Business, Sales & Funding, Product & Design, Technology, Skills & Management, Industries - who have made it big in their fields.

Schedule a Call!

We provide 3 different ways to connect to an Expert, using web call, video call or phone call. Select a date and time that works best for your schedule and specify a reason for the call. The expert has 72 hours to respond, or the request will expire.

Connect, Talk!

Go to the 'Live' page to make a call to the expert.After the call, you’ll be charged according to our per-minute rate, and they get paid. You can then leave a rating and review for the expert.
